Our Story
How Tekpair started

Honestly? We were broke students trying to find a real path forward. We both knew we wanted to build something — not just work a job. The first idea was a local event crew. That didn't pan out. But technology felt right. It was something we both understood, something people around us clearly needed help with, and something we could actually start with very little.

We built Tekpair with what we had left from work. We spent hours studying ads, figuring out what to post, doing outreach, putting up flyers across Cohoes and the Capital Region. No investors. No funding. Just two people grinding to make something out of nothing.

This is the result — our company. We're incredibly proud of what we've built and genuinely happy to serve our neighbors at a price that actually makes sense.

Community
Tekpair Gives Back
We collect what others don't need
At the end of every job, we ask if you have any old devices sitting around unused. No pressure — just a simple question.
We only take what we can restore
If we know a device can be fixed and given a second life, we take it. If it can't be restored without cost, we recycle it responsibly.
Wiped clean before it goes anywhere
Every device gets a full wipe and factory reset. Clean slate, no personal data — ready for whoever receives it.
Donated back into the community
Devices go directly to local families and individuals in the Capital Region who need them most.
Why this matters to us
Growing up, we both know what it's like to have less. We know firsthand how much a small gift — something as simple as a working computer — can mean to a kid or a family that doesn't have one. Tekpair Gives Back isn't a marketing campaign. It's just something we believe in doing.
