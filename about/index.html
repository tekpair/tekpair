Our Story

Built from the ground up—one problem at a time.

01

It almost started with an event cleanup crew.

Before Tekpair, we planned to launch C&K Event Crew, a Capital Region cleanup service for local events. The idea did not last long. We quickly realized the work was not for us—and that building a business around something we would eventually hate was probably not the smartest move.

02

Then came the idea that became Tekpair.

After weeks of throwing around business ideas, Connor asked a simple question: what if we created a local tech service for seniors and everyday people who needed help with common technology problems? Something more personal and accessible than a big-box support service, with affordable pricing and same-day availability. That idea became Tekpair in March 2026.

03

Residential support showed us everything — but sustainability.

Tekpair spent its first several months helping households throughout the Capital Region while also supporting small businesses such as Sweet Elevation Bakery. We loved showing up, solving problems, and giving people that feeling of relief when their technology finally worked again. But over time, we realized the residential model was not sustainable or leading us toward the business we wanted to build.

04

So we took what worked and built something bigger.

Connor proposed shifting Tekpair from residential support to commercial technology and growth services. The idea took some discussion, but we eventually agreed that it allowed us to keep doing what we enjoyed—solving real problems—on a larger and more sustainable scale. Today, Tekpair helps businesses build, improve, and support the technology behind their growth while continuing to offer the direct, personal service we started with.
