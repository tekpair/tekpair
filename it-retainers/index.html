How Support Works

Help without the ticket maze.

STEP 01

Tell us what's happening

You can reach Tekpair directly by emailing us or calling our business line. We start with the issue, who it affects, and how urgently it needs attention.

STEP 02

Troubleshooting directly

We work through the problem remotely when that is fastest, without passing you through layers of support or repeating the same explanation.

STEP 03

On-site support when needed

Hardware, networks, POS systems, and physical setups often need hands-on. Capital Region businesses get local support when the issue requires it.

STEP 04

Proactive guarantees

Regular check-ins help us spot aging equipment, recurring issues, security gaps, and smarter upgrades before they become emergencies.
