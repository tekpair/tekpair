Why a Retainer
What you get every month

A retainer turns unpredictable, reactive IT into a steady, proactive relationship. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Priority Support
You're at the front of the line. When something goes wrong, you reach a person who already knows your setup — no tickets lost in a queue.
Proactive Monitoring
We watch for problems before they become outages — updates, patches, and health checks handled so small issues never turn into downtime.
Network & Hardware
Wi-Fi, routers, printers, workstations, cameras, and more — we manage the equipment your business runs on so it just works.
Troubleshooting
From a single stubborn PC to a business-wide outage, we diagnose and fix the day-to-day tech problems that slow your team down.
Vendor Management
We deal with your software vendors, internet provider, and tech support lines so you don't have to sit on hold or translate the jargon.
On-Site Visits
Some things need hands on hardware. For Capital Region businesses, we come to you — included on the plans that need it.
Predictable Cost
One flat monthly rate. No surprise hourly bills when something breaks — budget your IT the same way every month.
Security & Backups
Sensible security practices, software patching, and backup checks to keep your data protected and recoverable.
Always Up to Date
Regular check-ins keep your systems current and give you a clear picture of what's working, what's aging, and what to plan for next.
