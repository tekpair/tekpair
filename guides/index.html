Using an ad blocker?

Please allow ads on Tekpair

Our guides are free and always will be. Ads are the only thing keeping this running. Your ad blocker is preventing them from loading.

uBlock Origin

  1. Click the uBlock Origin icon in your toolbar
  2. Click the large blue power button to disable for this site
  3. Click Reload when it appears

AdBlock / AdBlock Plus

  1. Click the AdBlock icon in your toolbar
  2. Select "Don't run on pages on this domain"
  3. Reload the page

Brave Browser (Shields)

  1. Click the Shields icon (lion) in the address bar
  2. Toggle Shields to off for tekpair.com
  3. The page will reload automatically

Other ad blockers

  1. Click your ad blocker icon in the toolbar
  2. Look for "Disable for this site", "Pause", or "Whitelist"
  3. Reload the page
