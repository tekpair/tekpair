Policies & Terms of Engagement

We keep things clear and professional. Here's how we scope projects, handle payments, and protect both sides throughout every engagement.

Projects

Project Engagement

Signed scope of work required
All projects begin with a signed scope of work that outlines deliverables, timelines, and pricing. No work begins until both parties have agreed in writing.
50% deposit before work begins
A 50% deposit is required before any project work begins. This secures your spot on our schedule and covers initial development costs.
Remaining balance due upon delivery
The remaining 50% is due upon project delivery. Final files and access are transferred once the balance is paid in full.
Scope changes require written agreement
Any changes to the original scope of work require written agreement from both parties and may adjust pricing and timelines accordingly.
Cancellations

Cancellation & Refunds

Cancel before work begins — full deposit refund
If you cancel before any work has started, your deposit is refunded in full. No questions asked.
Cancel after work has started — deposit covers completed work
If you cancel after work has begun, the deposit covers the work completed to date. You will receive all deliverables produced up to the point of cancellation.
Retainer cancellation — 30 days written notice
Retainer agreements require 30 days written notice to cancel. Services continue through the end of the notice period.
No refunds on completed and delivered work
Once work has been completed and delivered, no refunds are issued. We encourage feedback throughout the process to ensure satisfaction before final delivery.
Payment

Payment Terms

Invoiced via Square
All invoicing is handled through Square. We accept Card, ACH, and Zelle payments.
Net-15 terms for retainer clients
Net-15 payment terms are available for clients on active retainer agreements. All other invoices are due upon receipt.
Late payment interest
Invoices overdue by more than 30 days are subject to 1.5% monthly interest on the outstanding balance.
No payment collected on tekpair.com
Tekpair.com does not collect, process, or store any payment information. All invoicing and payment processing is handled exclusively through Square.
Ownership

Intellectual Property

Client owns all deliverables upon final payment
Once final payment is received, full ownership of all project deliverables transfers to the client.
Portfolio usage rights
Tekpair retains the right to showcase completed work in our portfolio and marketing materials unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.
Third-party assets are licensed, not owned
Any third-party assets used in your project (stock photos, fonts, plugins, etc.) are licensed under their respective terms. Ownership of these assets does not transfer — only the license to use them as part of your project.
Full delivery of source code and files
All source code, design files, and project assets are delivered in full upon project completion and final payment.
Limitations

Scope of Service

Services as described in scope of work
We provide services as described in the signed scope of work. Any work outside the agreed scope requires a separate agreement.
Hosting and third-party subscriptions
Hosting, domain registration, and third-party subscriptions are the client's responsibility unless explicitly agreed otherwise in the scope of work.
Third-party service outages
We are not responsible for outages or disruptions caused by third-party services, including hosting providers, APIs, payment processors, or other external platforms.
We'll always be upfront
If something falls outside our expertise, we'll tell you straight. We'd rather point you in the right direction than overpromise and underdeliver.
