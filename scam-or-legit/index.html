Golden Rules to Remember
No legitimate company will ever call you and ask for remote access to your computer.
If a pop-up tells you to call a phone number — don't. Power off your computer.
Urgency is a red flag. Scammers want you to act before you think.
Gift cards are never a valid payment method for any real company or government agency.
When in doubt — hang up, close the tab, and call the company directly using a number from their real website.
